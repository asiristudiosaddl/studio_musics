Asiri Perera is a Sri Lankan Sinhala artist and lyricist whose work blends emotion, culture, and modern sound. His music reflects real feelings, personal moments, and a unique creative voice that continues to grow across digital platforms.

My ✍️: A Mirror to Society

I create songs that speak deeply to people’s hearts. I base most of my work on the realities of everyday society. My goal is to craft something creative for the world, inspired by true events. My words may hurt you more than they comfort you — and because of that, I will become someone you will never forget, both as a person and as an artist.
